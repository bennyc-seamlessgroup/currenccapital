The Process

FIVE STEPS TO LIQUIDITY

From tokenized shares to stablecoins in your wallet.

  1. Tokenize

    Shares are converted to ERC-1400 tokens via Securitize.

  2. Connect

    The shareholder connects their wallet to a partner lending protocol.

  3. Deposit

    Tokenized shares are deposited as collateral.

  4. Borrow

    Borrow up to 30–40% LTV in USDC.

  5. Use & Repay

    Use USDC for any purpose. Repay anytime to reclaim your shares.
