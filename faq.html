How do I know if my company is under a short attack?

Watch for short interest above 30%, utilization above 70%, spiking cost-to-borrow, unusual dark pool volume, and coordinated negative media. Our Intelligence platform detects these signals 15–30 days before the damage becomes visible in your stock price.

Is tokenizing our shares SEC-compliant?

Yes. Tokenization is executed through Securitize — the first FINRA-approved broker-dealer for tokenized securities, with a registered ATS and over $1B tokenized. ERC-1400 security tokens carry programmable, auto-enforced compliance.

Do we need to tokenize all of our shares?

No. Most companies start with insider and treasury blocks. Every share you tokenize is a share removed from the global lending pool — reducing the ammunition available to short sellers. Each weapon is modular and can be deployed independently.

How fast can we deploy?

Intelligence + Tokenization go live in 4 weeks from signed engagement. Lending protocols add 2–3 weeks, DEX listing 3–4 weeks, and IR & Market Making runs as an ongoing engagement.

What if we can't spare the cash right now?

We offer a no-cash-outlay option — pay in shares at the current market price. Companies under attack need every dollar for operations; our pricing is built around that reality.

Does a DEX listing affect our NASDAQ listing?

No — it's complementary. Your NASDAQ listing continues unchanged while tokenized shares trade 24/7 on Jupiter. Jump Trading's cross-market arbitrage keeps pricing in parity between venues.
