Weapon 03

C-Lending (DeFi)

The Problem: Shareholders needed liquidity but did not want to sell. Traditional stock loan institutions sold their pledged shares into the market, further crushing the price.

The Solution: DeFi lending that lets shareholders pledge tokenized shares as collateral to borrow stablecoins — without shares ever touching the open market.

  • Unlock liquidity WITHOUT selling — no price pressure
  • Pledged shares locked in smart contracts
  • No margin calls, no forced liquidations
  • No hypothecation — structural vulnerability eliminated
  • Access to Aave, Kamino, Morpho protocols
"Traditional stock loans sell pledged shares and destroy your price. Our DeFi lending keeps shares locked — liquidity without the damage."
