Investor Requirements

WHO CAN TRADE, AND HOW IT'S ENFORCED

For investors trading tokenized shares on Jupiter — enforced through Securitize iD and smart contracts.

Investor Requirements

  • KYC / AML via Securitize iD
  • Accredited investor (Reg D) or non-US investor (Reg S)
  • Eligible securities wallet
  • DTC-registered digital wallet

Token-Level Compliance

Auto-Block Non-KYC Transfers

Smart contract checks the Securitize iD registry before allowing any transfer. Non-verified wallets are automatically blocked.

Holding Period Enforcement

Reg S Category 3 requires a 1-year holding period — enforced at the protocol level, with no exceptions possible.

Jurisdiction Restrictions

Tokens can be restricted by investor jurisdiction — US investors are automatically blocked from Reg S tokens, all enforced on-chain.

Accreditation Per Transfer

Every transfer verifies investor accreditation status in real time. Lapsed accreditation blocks the transfer.
